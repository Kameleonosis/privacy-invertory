How buttons work (quick help)
  • Refresh: reloads records from local storage.
  • Export JSON: downloads your local records as a JSON backup.
  • Import CSV: imports sites from a password manager/Chrome export (adds as EMAIL entries).
  • Clear all: deletes ALL local records (cannot be undone).
  • Open (per site): opens the details drawer for that domain.
  • Mark as deleted on site: sets status to “USER_DELETED_ON_SITE” for that site’s types.
  • Open deletion help: shows quick links and steps to remove your data on that site.
  • Delete from history (local): removes that site’s records from this extension only.
  • Save notes: stores your notes locally for that domain.
